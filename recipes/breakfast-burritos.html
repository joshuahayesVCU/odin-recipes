Breakfast Burritos

Yummy breakfast burritos are great to make ahead of time and toss into the air fryer in the morning when your looking for a filling breakfast and only have time for something quick. But with the price of eggs?

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Shredded cheese
  • Half a ripe avacado
  • Sausage links
  • Tater tots

Recipe

  1. Heat pan over medium heat until hot. Add a pad of butter to the pan, allowing to melt and coat the surface eavenly.
  2. Add the sausage to the pan, making sure to turn ocassionaly to cook all the way through.
  3. While the sausage is cooking, slice the avacado, whisk eggs in a bowl with seasoning, and air fry tatter tots.
  4. When the sausage has cooked all the way though, place onto paper towel. Allow the pan to heat up before melting another pad of butter.
  5. Add the eggs to the pan and scramble. You want the eggs to be a little runny when taking them off heat. Otherwise, they will go dry in the air fryer.
  6. Assemble burritos with sausage, eggs, cheese, avocado. Roll and place in air fryer. Air fry until golden brown.
