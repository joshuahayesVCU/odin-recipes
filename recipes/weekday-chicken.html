Weekday Chicken

Poorly cooked chicken is dry, tasteless, and hard to eat. The recipe below outlines the steps to create a juicy and well seasoned chicken that pairs well with veggies and rice to make a full weekday dinner meal.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Seasonings of choice

Recipe

  1. Butterfly your chicken by sclicing it evenly through the middle of the breast "hamburger style" to thin the breast and allow for faster cooking
  2. Season generously with salt, pepper, and seasonings of choice. Lay a sheet of tin foil across the entire breast and beat with a meat tenderizer to further flatten and even the chicken.
  3. Heat a pan to medium heat. When pan is hot, add a 2tb drizzle of olive oil into the pan and allow it to heat up before adding the chicken. Cook about 4-5 minutes on each side. Avoid flipping multiple times.
  4. Add a tablespoon of butter to the pan and allow it to melt. Flip the chicken in the butter to coat.
  5. Allow the chicken to cool after removing from pan and before cutting into it to allow the juices to settle. Serve warm
